# Grill me

Interview me relentlessly about every aspect of this plan until we reach a shared understanding. Walk down each branch of the decision tree, resolving dependencies between decisions one-by-one. For each question, provide your recommended answer.

Ask the questions one at a time.

If a question can be answered by exploring the codebase, explore the codebase instead.

**Usage:**
- `/user:grill-me` — when you want your plan or design challenged before implementing
- Say "grill me on this" to trigger this mode on any topic

---

**Scope:** Challenge requirements, architecture decisions, edge cases, failure modes, scalability assumptions, security implications, and operational concerns. Do not stop until every major branch of the design is resolved.
